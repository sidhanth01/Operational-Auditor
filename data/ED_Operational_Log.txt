Emergency Department Operational Summary – Q1 2025
Filed by: ED Operations Manager

Patient Volume:
ED visits increased by 12% compared to Q4.

Wait Time Analysis:
Average wait time increased from 50 minutes in Q4
to 55 minutes in Q1.

Monthly breakdown:
January: 52 minutes
February: 60 minutes
March: 53 minutes

Night shifts recorded peak wait times up to 70 minutes,
particularly during high-volume weekends.

Operational Factors:
Temporary nurse absences and increased patient inflow
contributed to throughput delays.
