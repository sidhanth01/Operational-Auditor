Subject: Night Shift Staffing Concern
From: ED Supervisor
Date: March 15, 2025

During February and early March,
the emergency department experienced a shortage
of 8–10 nurses per night shift.

Although regulatory ratios appear compliant on paper,
actual on-floor availability during peak hours
has been insufficient.

This shortage contributed to longer wait times
and increased staff fatigue.

Immediate review recommended.
