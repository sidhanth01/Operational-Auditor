Green Valley Multi-Specialty Hospital
Executive Performance Report – Q1 2025
Prepared by: Executive Office
Date: April 2, 2025

1. Summary

Q1 2025 reflects measurable improvements across patient satisfaction,
operational efficiency, and compliance performance.

2. Patient Satisfaction

Total survey responses: 1,000
Overall satisfaction increased by 15% compared to Q4 2024.
Average score improved from 4.0/5 to 4.6/5.

Emergency department satisfaction improved from 3.9 to 4.2.

3. Emergency Department Performance

Average emergency department wait time decreased
from 50 minutes in Q4 to 42 minutes in Q1,
representing a 16% improvement.

4. Infection Control

Overall infection control compliance rate: 97%.
No major or repeated hygiene violations were identified
during scheduled audits.

5. Staffing

All departments maintained regulatory nurse-to-patient ratios.
No department was formally classified as understaffed.

Conclusion:
Q1 performance demonstrates operational stability and
improvement across key hospital metrics.
