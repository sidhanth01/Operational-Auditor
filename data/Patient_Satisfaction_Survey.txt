Patient Experience Survey – Q1 2025

Total responses: 1,000
Confidence level: 95%

Overall satisfaction score increased by 15% compared to Q4.
Average rating improved from 4.0 to 4.6.

Emergency department rating improved from 3.9 to 4.2.

Top positive feedback themes:
- Friendly and attentive doctors
- Clean and well-maintained rooms
- Clear discharge explanations

Some patients mentioned wait time frustration,
but overall perception of care quality improved.
